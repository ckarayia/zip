CALL FOR ARTISTS ~ ZIP IS BACK!


For our third installment, we are proud to announce this year’s theme:

PASS–PORTALS: REPRESENTATION AND IDENTITY ACROSS DIGITAL REALMS

The struggle to gain citizenship in America, where it was once the land of possibilities, has become increasingly difficult.

Our goal is to showcase a group of national and international artists to create a dialog of identity and what it means to be human in the twenty first century amidst an age of technology and non-physical internet representation.

We will be representing art from a variety of mediums, not regularly seen beyond the non-physical sphere of the internet. We hope that artists displayed in conjunction with each other will open a conversation on the value of inclusivity, collaboration and support.



DEADLINE: 15TH FEB 2018



EVENT DATE : 9TH–10TH MAR 2018



ELIGIBILITY

- Artists working in all medium are encouraged to apply!

- Artwork on paper and photographs must be framed.

- Artist living outside NYC are encouraged to apply, however you will be responsible for artwork shipping costs and it is highly encouraged for you to be able to attend the show.

- Artists working in new media, film, or video - may be required to use your own equipment.

- OPTIONAL $25 SUBMISSION DONATION

From the beginning, Zip has been committed to creating a platform to support and showcase artists. The organization does not make a profit. The submission donation we have implemented will be going into the provided cost of the venue, installation, t-shirt printing, photographer and videographer

In addition, 10% of the money collected will be donated to The Lower East Side Girls Club.

The Lower East Side Girls Club is A community center which offers resources and a safe space with programs in the arts, sciences, leadership, entrepreneurship, and wellness for girls in middle and high school.

BENEFITS

- We will provide you with representation and a platform to showcase your work

- Your name and work will be featured on our website and social media

- We will be making a full video documenting the show

- Professional photography of yourself and your work

- Your name will be printed on our official Zip art show t–shirts

- You will have the opportunity to sell your work at the show [20% commission fee]



*After clicking on the submit link you will be directed to paypal where you will be asked to donate the optional $25 submission fee. Then you will be redirected to page with details on how to submit.
